CONFERENCE:

Title: Secure Crime Record Sharing Using Cryptography and Steganography

Description:
                   I had the privilege of presenting my research at Rajalakshmi Engineering College on "Secure Crime Record Sharing Using Cryptography and Steganography." This innovative project addresses the critical need for secure and confidential sharing of crime records among law enforcement agencies and stakeholders. By integrating advanced cryptographic techniques like AES and RSA for data encryption and leveraging steganography methods such as LSB insertion, our solution ensures that sensitive information remains hidden within digital media, thereby enhancing confidentiality and thwarting unauthorized access. The presentation highlighted our system's capability to securely transmit and retrieve encrypted crime data while preserving data integrity, contributing significantly to advancements in secure information sharing practices.
